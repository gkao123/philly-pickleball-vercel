# Where To Play

This page lists all the places to play pickleball in Philadelphia and beyond. Work in progress! Please feel free to add more places here by submitting a pull request or messaging me on IG.


### Philadelphia
FDR Park \
Address: Delaware Expy, Philadelphia, PA 19145 \
Open plays every morning and Monday, Wednesday, Friday evenings

Segar Park \
Address: 1020 Lombard St, Philadelphia, PA 19147 \
Open plays listed on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/philadelphia-playing-pickleball-table-tennis-meetup/)

East Passyunk Community Center \
Address: 1025 Mifflin St, Philadelphia, PA 19148 \
Open plays Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, listed on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/east-passyunk-community-center-pickleball-meetup-group/events)

Water Tower Recreation Center \
Address: 8139 Ardleigh St, Philadelphia, PA 19118 \
Open plays usually every day when the weather is warm

### South Jersey
Moorestown Tennis Club \
Address: 15 Twosome Dr, Moorestown, NJ 08057 \
Open plays listed on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/southjerseypickleball/events)

### Main Line

Dink City Pickleball \
Address: 800 Radnor Street Road, Wayne, PA, USA \
Open plays done through their site
